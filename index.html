This dress code adherence will be enforced with immediate effect very strictly. If a student is found violating this code of conduct, he will not be allowed to enter the college. All College Rules & Regulations are to be followed very strictly. All students are hereby instructed that Cell Phones are totally prohibited in the College Campus with immediate effect. If any student is found possessing the Cell Phones, it will be confiscated and will not be returned at all. As per the official order from Government of India, Government of Andhra Pradesh and directive from the District Collector, Karimnagar, all students should possess valid student ID card issued by the College and should bring along with them the ID cards always when coming to the College. No Student is allowed to enter the College without the valid student ID card. They should produce the student ID card to the members of the College Disciplinary Committee, Anti-Ragging Committee and Police Officials on demand. The Student not carrying valid IDs will be sent out of the college without any notice. Condonation of shortage of attandance in aggregate up to 10%(65% above and below 75%) in each semester or I year may be granted by the college Acadamic Committee on geninue and valid reasons on representation by the candidate with supporting evidance (medical grounds). 3. A student will not be pramoted to the next semester unless he/she satiifies the attandance requirement of the present semester / I year when offered next. 4. Shortage of attendance below 65% in aggregate shall in NO case be condoned. 5. Students whose shortage of attendance is not condoned in any semester / I year are not eligible to take their end examination of that class and their regsitration shall stand cancelled. 6. A stipulated fee shall be payable towards condonation of shortage of attendance. 1.A regular student should attend all classes and laboratory sessions. A student may be discontinued from a course and denied entrance to the final examination if his/her attendance is less than the limit determined by the University Council. 2. A candidate shall be deemed to have eligibility to write end semester examinations in a subject if he/his has put in atleast 75% of attendance in that subject. 3. Shortage of attendance upto 10% in any subject ( i.e., 65% and above and below 75%) may be condoned by the College Academic Committee on genuine and valid reasons on representation by the candidate with supporting evidence. 4. A candidate shall get minimum required attendance atleast in three (3) theory subjects to get promoted to the next semester. 5. Shortage of attendance below 65% shall no case be condoned. 6. A Stipulated fee shall be payable towards condonation of shortage of attendance. All the Heads of the Departments and Faculty Members are hereby informed to note that the cumulative monthly attendance of the students has to be sent to the University every month. Hence they are required to submit the monthly cummulative attendance report by 1st of every month so that it can be sent to the University before 5th of that month after proper verification. The faculty members should inform the students that they should attend more than 75% of the class every month so that their aggregate attendance will be more than 75% before the end of the Semester.
